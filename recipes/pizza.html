Pizza Recipe

Description:

A delicious pizza materialized by the power of magic that doesn't exist.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Tomato sauce
  • Toppings - various
  • Magic sprinkle dust

Steps

  1. Roll out dough onto pizza pan
  2. Spread tomato sauce onto dough
  3. Top with toppings
  4. Sprinkle generously with magic sprinkle dust
